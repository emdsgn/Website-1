Company

DPS Original is a two-division full-service marketing, event strategy and healthcare education agency.

At DPS Original and DPS Original Healthcare, our passion is strategic marketing, creative branding, event strategy, executive & advisory committee meetings and healthcare education. Our experience, which averages 15 years of partnering with major clients, goes beyond the traditional and expected and delivers the most effective, strategic and practical marketing, event and health education solutions. We have the passion, experience, skills, promotional, marketing, facilitating, event management, and selling proficiencies that make the outreach and education programs the most effective, efficient and successful.

Who We Are

With over 15 years of solid marketing, executive meeting and event strategy experience, Dennis brings strong strategic counsel to both corporate clients and agency personnel.

Dennis’ career started at Putnam Associates in Boston ranked among the US’ top 50 Management Consulting firms, where he provided strategic marketing consulting solutions for Fortune 500 (global pharmaceutical and consumer companies) and healthcare associations. His marketing and business responsibilities rapidly expanded and ultimately landed an offer as a strategic Marketing Communications consultant and key account lead for Pfizer Canada. After lead agency consulting roles in Boston, Montreal and Toronto, Dennis took on senior marketing management roles leading marketing teams and motivating sales teams to launch new technologies and blockbuster, innovative products across North America. At the same time, he would also specialize in event strategy and facilitating strategic advisory boards meetings for healthcare professionals and marketing teams throughout Europe, the US and Canada.

From there, Dennis decided to combine his extensive marketing proficiency with his strategic event and meetings experience to form DPS Original and DPS Original Healthcare a two-division full-service marketing and event strategy company. Through Dennis’ leadership, DPS Original is rapidly growing with the goal of becoming one of North America’s leading marketing, event strategy and health education agencies.

Dennis is proud to continue to share his marketing knowledge as a Strategic Marketing Advisor for Canada’s entrepreneurs at Invest Ottawa encouraging them to achieve the highest professional standards, and he is also a visiting lecturing Professor at the University of Ottawa’s Telfer School of Business Management. Dennis holds a Masters of International Marketing from the University of Strathclyde (Glasgow, Scotland, UK); a Bachelor of Commerce (Marketing) from Mount Allison University (Sackville, NB, Canada) and he has authored 4 publications in marketing journals and literature.

    We take on facilitating meetings strategically, with an unprecedented energy level guaranteed to engage any audience. At DPS Original, our meeting facilitation services can help your team work towards one common goal, all the while improving participation and fostering open dialogue.
