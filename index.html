Company

DPS Original is comprised of two companies: DPS Original Healthcare and DPS Original Marketing & Design.

At DPS Original Healthcare and DPS Marketing & Design, our passion is medical education, workshops & training, strategic marketing and creative branding. Our 15 years of experience partnering with major companies and innovative organizations, goes beyond the traditional and expected, delivering the most effective, strategic and practical solutions.

Partners

What We Bring

We offer innovative solutions in medical education, workshops & training, and marketing & design.

By combining global communications, scientific research, and state-of-the-art innovation, DPS Original Healthcare delivers the finest thought-leader-focused medical- education services in the industry. DPS Original Healthcare identifies, creates, and nurtures relationships with the world's leading scientists, researchers, academics, and physicians in all areas of healthcare. DPS Original Healthcare is fully equipped with seasoned medical-education professionals. Our management team has extensive experience integrating traditional and nontraditional medical-education activities for deployment pre-launch, at launch, and postlaunch.

Moreover, DPS Marketing & Design has a keen eye for creating the most effective, creative and unique marketing strategies and designs. Backed with several years of experience developing brands from the ground up, DPS Marketing & Design gives every brand a creative edge. Working with Fortune 500 companies, to small start-up companies, our record is one of leadership in brand development and design.

How We Do It

We leverage the insights of the thought-leader community to craft dynamic, integrated communications plans.

As medical-education experts, one of our specialties is helping clients build unique messaging from emerging science. We leverage the insights of the thought-leader community to craft dynamic, integrated communications plans that combine exceptional clinical understanding with strategic savvy. With a strong scientific platform in place, we then help coordinate medical-education activities and integrate them into your overall product positioning. Our strategic medical-education initiatives offer highly effective solutions for established brands, as well as for prelaunch products in later stages of development.

How do our marketing minds make it better? With our rigorous, disciplined brand enhancement process. By combining our years of practical working marketing experiences, driving and identifying the most modern marketing strategies for blockbuster brands such as Celebrex, Enbrel and Enfamil, and emerging brands such as HalTech, Inspire and Nitor. In summary, working with various levels of management, we take traditional creative design 10 steps further.

Leadership Team

DPS has multiple capabilities.

  • Dennis Sakalauskas

    President & Founder

    With over 15 years leading global marketing, medical education initiatives and national sales teams, Dennis brings strong strategic counsel to both corporate clients and agency personnel. His unique medical education, marketing and design strategies have been effectively implemented by some of the world's largest companies, including Pfizer, BMS, TDL Limited and Medtronic, to help elevate major brands such as Celebrex, Enbrel, the Enfamil portfolio and seven different global blockbuster product launches. As the Founder and President of DPS Original, he continues to lead his marketing and medical education agency teams to drive the most effective strategies and supporting tactics and programs for major biopharmaceutical, Fortune 500, and emerging start-up companies. Dennis and his teams have led over 450 corporate strategic planning workshops, marketing strategy planning sessions, and medical education and strategic advisory board meetings. He continues to contribute back to several communities, providing supporting talks and seminars to future business leaders. He is a visiting guest lecturer at various universities across North America, and is a published author in journals such as the Journal of EuroMarketing and the book Internationalization. Dennis holds a Masters of International Marketing from the University of Strathclyde (Scotland, UK) and a Bachelor of Commerce from Mount Allison University (Canada).

  • Ian Murchison

    Partner

    With a product design background from handheld electronics, to bicycle racks and medical robotics, Ian Murchison is an award winning designer with a wide portfolio. As a founder of a successful design studio, The Federal Inc., Ian has used his experience to branch farther in to new design markets, exploring new possibilities with form and materials. Ian has received a Bachelor of Industrial Design from Carleton University as was a former designer at BlackBerry. Ian works to combine the end goal, user experience, and innovation together as one, no matter the product, service or scenario.

  • Rohan Thakar

    Partner

    Rohan Thakar has been recognized as an award winning product designer and boasts a portfolio of successful market products. With a strong drive towards design research and thinking, his design sense spreads well beyond just the physical components. Rohan is a founder of the successful design studio, The Federal Inc., with a BID from Carleton University. Rohan was previously a designer on the accessory design team at BlackBerry in Waterloo. Rohans passion for good design is based upon a need to make products and services better for generations to come.

  • Mansura Chowdhury

    Lead Design & Web Developer

    Short, creative designer with an eye for user experience and pixel perfection. Mansura's insight into the web inspires and educates her, while also keeping her employer's clients projects user friendly and clean with meaningful deisgn solutions. She is always trying her best to combine her skills and knowledge to deliver the best creative solutions to the clients and their audience. Mansura is always looking to actively learn and expand her skills as a proud graduate from Algonquin College in Graphic design.
