Company

DPS ORGINAL IS COMPRISED OF TWO COMPANIES: DPS Original Healthcare and DPS Original Marketing & Design.

At DPS Original Healthcare and DPS Marketing & Design, our passion is medical education, workshops & training, strategic marketing and creative branding. Our 15 years of experience partnering with major companies and innovative organizations, goes beyond the traditional and expected, delivering the most effective, strategic and practical solutions.

Why DPS

DPS has multiple capabilities.

What We Bring

We offer innovative solutions in medical education, workshops & training, and marketing & design.

By combining global communications, scientific research, and state-of-the-art innovation, DPS Original Healthcare delivers the finest thought-leader-focused medical- education services in the industry. DPS Original Healthcare identifies, creates, and nurtures relationships with the world's leading scientists, researchers, academics, and physicians in all areas of healthcare. DPS Original Healthcare is fully equipped with seasoned medical-education professionals. Our management team has extensive experience integrating traditional and nontraditional medical-education activities for deployment pre-launch, at launch, and postlaunch.

Moreover, DPS Marketing & Design has a keen eye for creating the most effective, creative and unique marketing strategies and designs. Backed with several years of experience developing brands from the ground up, DPS Marketing & Design gives every brand a creative edge. Working with Fortune 500 companies, to small start-up companies, our record is one of leadership in brand development and design.

How We Do It

We leverage the insights of the thought-leader community to craft dynamic, integrated communications plans.

As medical-education experts, one of our specialties is helping clients build unique messaging from emerging science. We leverage the insights of the thought-leader community to craft dynamic, integrated communications plans that combine exceptional clinical understanding with strategic savvy. With a strong scientific platform in place, we then help coordinate medical-education activities and integrate them into your overall product positioning. Our strategic medical-education initiatives offer highly effective solutions for established brands, as well as for prelaunch products in later stages of development.

How do our marketing minds make it better? With our rigorous, disciplined brand enhancement process. By combining our years of practical working marketing experiences, driving and identifying the most modern marketing strategies for blockbuster brands such as Celebrex, Enbrel and Enfamil, and emerging brands such as HalTech, Inspire and Nitor. In summary, working with various levels of management, we take traditional creative design 10 steps further.

Partners

Our past and present working relationships include some of the world's largest companies, as well as partnerships with some emerging, innovative companies.

Leadership

Dennis Sakalauskas

President

Leadership and Team Management
Over 15 years combined Management Consulting, Marketing Leadership and ‎Account Leads among the US and Canada's top ranked firms, including Putnam Associates (Boston) and Inventiv Health (Montreal and Toronto) and directly for corporate brands for some of the world's largest companies, such as Pfizer, Bristol-Myers Squibb, TDL Limited and Medtronic. Dennis has led teams from pre-launch to market, as well as for well established brands including years of leading extensive sales teams on global marketing and medical education initiatives throughout the US, Canada and Europe. Dennis brings strong strategic counsel to both corporate clients and agency personnel. Most importantly, from starting out on his first job out on the bakery and retail floors during his adolescent years, Dennis' leadership is based on hardwork, passion and dedication to all of his represented customers and clients for all brands.

Marketing, Brand Development and Product Launches
Dennis has led several marketing and sales teams for leading established brands‎ across many industries, including the biopharmaceutical, medical device and consumer goods. Companies who have benefited from his marketing leadership and medical education work include Pfizer, Bristol-Myers Squibb, Medtronic and TDL Limited.

Dennis has driven marketing and education strategies for 7 different global blockbuster product launches on both the account and client sides.‎ By combining his 15 years of solid marketing and facilitating experiences with his medical education proficiency, Dennis brings a unique strategic edge to pre-launch brands and established brands‎.

Training and Facilitating
Dennis has been asked to present marketing and selling strategies and organizational direction to over 100 sales teams at national sales meetings (including major product launches) across the US, Canada and Europe. His unique, dynamic style has led him to be a keynote speaker for the International Association of Business Communicators. He is regularly invited to present marketing, coaching and education seminars to future business leaders, as well as ongoing supporting talks to financially challenged business members in the community. Dennis has facilitated and led well over 450 corporate workshops and strategic business meetings. Clients and organizations who have benefited from his facilitating expertise include Pfizer, Medtronic, Bristol-Myers Squibb, Fujisawa, Novartis, Schering-Plough, Wyeth-Ayerst (now part of Pfizer), Amgen, OPH, Princess Margaret Cancer Centre, SickKids Hospital, Boston Atrial Fibrillation Conference, Capital Housing Staging and Design, Heart Rhythm Society annual meeting, the American Academy of Pediatrics, and the Canadian Paediatric Society.

Mentorship
Dennis continues to provide ongoing consultation in the community and supporting talks to future business leaders. In addition to providing leadership to the team at DPS Original Healthcare and DPS Marketing & Design, Dennis is a Part-time Guest Lecturing Professor for courses such as Management in the New Economy, and Marketing Services at the University of Ottawa's Telfer School of Management. He has been invited as a guest speaker at universities in North America and Europe. Dennis has also coached major university business competition teams. Since 2011, Dennis has presented to over 750 university students, and future enterpreneurs and business leaders from across North America.

Dennis graduated with a Masters of International Marketing from the University of Strathclyde in Glasgow, Scotland, UK (2000) and with a Bachelor of Commerce from Mount Allison University in New Brunswick, Canada (1998). He has published 4 peer reviewed articles in publications such as the Journal of EuroMarketing and a chapter in the book Internationalization.
