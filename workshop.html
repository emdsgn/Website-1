Workshop & Facilitating

OUR PASSION IS NOT JUST TO GAIN ALIGNMENT, BUT ALSO TO MOTIVATE, ENERGIZE AND COACH TEAMS TO BETTER UNDERSTAND THE STRATEGY and THE KEY MESSAGES.

You've got two days, one day or even half a day, and one meeting room to do it all: cover multiple agendas, handle a cross-functional team of unique personalities, and develop prioritized and actionable items that ensure alignment within the group. At DPS Original, our meeting facilitation services can help you win the hearts and minds of your cross-functional team.

No matter what type of meeting – strategic, logical, or creative – DPS Original’s professional facilitation services are tailored to meet your needs. Our disciplined approach to meeting facilitation ensures you achieve the day's objectives while building trust and a sense of collective ownership among team members.

Training

Our training programs are only results-oriented.

A DPS Original training programs will find the right way to engage and challenge new and experienced sales professionals, sales directors or medical staff.

We design unique and interactive sessions that are both evocative and provocative. We take participants out of their comfort zones and engage them to look at your brand in a different way, leading to actionable marketing intelligence.

Unique to DPS Original, we also follow-up once programs are implemented to ensure established results are being achieved.

Strategic Planning

We challenge our clients to take a hard look at the brand.

Our dynamic approach is a blend of strategic and creative thinking while our disciplined process is both a science and an art inspired by non-conformity.

We work with our clients to create the most effective strategic planning meetings to meet their challenges. This includes:

  • An approach that starts with the end goal in mind
  • Change management to effectively transform an organization to high performance
  • Provide actionable performance information to better inform decision making
  • Strategic initiatives to focus attention on the most important performance improvement projects
  • Align strategy and culture, with a focus on results and the drivers of results
  • Integrate existing organization systems and align the organization around the strategy
