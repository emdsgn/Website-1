Workshop & Facilitating

Our passion is not just to gain alignment but also to motivate, energize and coach teams to better understand the strategy, but also the key messages.

You've got one day and one meeting room to do it all: address multiple agendas, handle a cross-functional team of different personalities, and develop prioritized and actionable items that ensure alignment within the group. At DPS Original, our meeting facilitation services can help you win the hearts and minds of your cross-functional team.

No matter what type of meeting – strategic, logical, or creative – DPS Original’s professional facilitation services are tailored to meet your needs. Our disciplined approach to meeting facilitation ensures you achieve the day's objectives while building trust and a sense of collective ownership among team members.

Training

Our training programs are only results-oriented.

A DPS Original sales training program will find better ways to engage and challenge new and experienced sales professionals, sales directors and medical staff.

We design unique and interactive sessions that are both evocative and provocative. We take participants out of their comfort zones and engage them to look at your brand in a different way, leading to actionable marketing intelligence.

Specific to DPS Original, we also follow-up after programs are implemented to ensure results are truly being achieved.

Strategic Planning

We challenge our clients to take a hard look at the brand.

Our disciplined approach is a blend of strategic and creative thinking while our process is both a science and an art; grounded in discipline, inspired by non-conformity.

We work with our clients to create the most effective strategic planning meetings to meet those challenges.
